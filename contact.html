Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the NovaBuk platform.

Is my health data secure with NovaBuk?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end industry-standard encryption for data storage and transfer. Our operations align with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) and HIPAA guidelines to guarantee complete patient privacy.

How do clinics get onboarded?

Onboarding is quick and easy. Simply click the "Join Our Pilot Clinics" button, fill in the interest form, and our support team will reach out to help migrate your files and train your staff in less than 48 hours.

Is NovaBuk free for patients to use?

Yes, patient accounts are free forever. You can log personal symptoms, view medical histories shared by your clinic, and book appointments without any charges.

Does NovaBuk work offline?

Yes, NovaBuk includes offline capabilities. Clinics can continue to log patient details and visits during connectivity drops, and the records will synchronize securely with the cloud once the network is restored.
